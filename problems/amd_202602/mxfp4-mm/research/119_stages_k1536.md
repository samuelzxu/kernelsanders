# #119 num_stages=2 for K=1536

## Change
K=1536 M_LEQ_64 and M_LEQ_256: num_stages=3→2

## Results
M=256: 20.2µs (worse than #118's 19.8µs)
num_stages=3 is optimal for 3 K-iterations.

## Pipeline stage analysis
| K-iters | num_stages=2 | num_stages=3 | num_stages=4 |
|---------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| 3 | OK | **Best** | Too deep |
| 4 | Insufficient | OK | **Best** (#118) |
| 7 | Too shallow | **Best** | Worse (#117) |

Optimal: num_stages ≈ min(K_iters, 4). More than K_iters wastes registers.
