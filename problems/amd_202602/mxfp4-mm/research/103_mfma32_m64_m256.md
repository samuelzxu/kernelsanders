# #103 MFMA 32x32 for M=64 AND M=256

## Changes
Extended MFMA32 from M=256 only (#102) to also include M=64 K=2048.

## Results (Ranked)
- M=64: 22.4µs (WORSE +2.4µs vs #102's 20.0µs) ❌
- M=256: 20.0µs (slightly worse than #102's 19.5µs)
- Geomean: ~16.6µs (worse than #102's ~15.9µs)

## Conclusion
MFMA32 is BAD for M=64 with BSM=32 BSN=64. Confirmed from both #101 and #103.
The BSM=32 tile creates exactly one 32x32 MFMA in M - less flexibility for
the compiler to schedule compute/memory overlap vs four 16x16 tiles.

MFMA32 summary:
- M=256 K=1536: MFMA32 helps ~0.5-1µs (BSM=64 = two 32x32 tiles)
- M=64 K=2048: MFMA32 hurts ~2µs (BSM=32 = one 32x32 tile, bad)
- M=32 K=512: MFMA32 neutral or slightly worse

Keep #102 (MFMA32 only for M_LEQ_256 in K=1536).
