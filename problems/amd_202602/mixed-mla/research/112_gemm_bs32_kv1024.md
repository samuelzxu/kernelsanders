# Attempt 112: GEMM for bs<=32/kv<=1024 - NEW BEST

## Strategy
- bs<=32 AND kv<=1024: bf16 GEMM (matmul + softmax + matmul)
- Everything else: assembly kernels (a16w8 or a8w8)

## Results
| Batch | KV | Method | Benchmark | Ranked |
|-------|----|--------|-----------|--------|
| 4 | 1024 | GEMM | 25.5 | 28.5 |
| 4 | 8192 | a8w8+Triton | 41.5 | 43.6 |
| 32 | 1024 | GEMM | 41.5 | 46.8 |
| 32 | 8192 | a8w8+Triton | 88.5 | 90.5 |
| 64 | 1024 | a16w8 | 46.6 | 50.3 |
| 64 | 8192 | a8w8+Triton | 143 | 144 |

Benchmark geomean: ~67 µs (was ~73, -8%)

## Why GEMM wins for small cases
- No metadata computation (saves ~8 µs GPU kernel)
- No metadata buffer allocation (saves ~2 µs)
- No torch.arange for kvi (saves ~2 µs)
- No reduce step (saves ~7 µs)
- Just 3 kernels: matmul + softmax + matmul
- hipBLASLt is very efficient for small GEMMs
